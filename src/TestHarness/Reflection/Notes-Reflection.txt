System.Collections
------------------
IEnumerable - Exposes an enumerator, which supports a simple iteration over a non-generic collection.
ICollection : System.Collections.IEnumerable

IDictionary : System.Collections.ICollection
IList : System.Collections.ICollection

System.Collections.Generic
--------------------------
IEnumerable<out <T> : System.Collections.IEnumerable
ICollection<T> : System.Collections.Generic.IEnumerable<T>

IDictionary<TKey,TValue> : ICollection<KeyValuePair<TKey,TValue>>, IEnumerable<KeyValuePair<TKey,TValue>>
IList<T> : ICollection<T>, IEnumerable<T>
